5" Race

Light, fast, and agile for tight turns and fast movements

Race quads are usually built up to a specified standard for bigger, more official races, or no-standard for smaller races/practice.
You will often see taller, high kv motors. 2207, 2207.5, 2208, 1800-2000kv paired with 20x20 stacks, lower outpt VTX's and low latency cameras and radio systems.

This list will include both standards, from budget to premium. I haven't included cameras/radio systems, as those are largely up to preference.
Prices may depend on where you source the parts. (Prices and specs are sourced from the manufacturers website when possible)
These are not affiliate links for your, and my convenience.
